About

Built by traders, for traders and the firms that serve them.

Excelsior Consultancy is a UK firm that connects the world stock market to private clients and businesses through cloud servers and systematic strategies. The team has been running real capital since 2023. Every claim made on this site is published with numbers behind it.

Who we are

A small firm with one job, done properly.

No salespeople, no call centres, no pressure. The people who run the strategies are the same people you speak to on the discovery call.

House style

Editorial, not promotional

We publish what we use. Maximum drawdown, win rate, profit factor, slippage assumptions. The number a marketing team would hide is the one we lead with.

Operating principle

Systems over opinions

Mechanical rules executed the same way every time. Opinions move when markets move. Disciplined systems do not.

UK consultancy

Global brokers, UK base

Registered and operated from the UK. Clients are connected to brokers in the US, Europe, and elsewhere via cloud servers. The infrastructure is global; the accountability is local.

Flagship product

Algo by Excelsior

The firm's published trading system, with verified performance figures and a public specification. One of several services, but the most-asked-about. See the product site for the full numbers.

Principles

How we work, regardless of who's paying.

  1. 01

    Publish or don't claim

    Any performance figure on this site or any other site we run is verifiable. If a number can't be backed by a broker statement or an audited backtest, it doesn't appear.

  2. 02

    Brief before build

    Every engagement begins with a written brief. Deliverables, timelines, and pricing are agreed before anything starts. Scope creep is a planning failure, not a feature.

  3. 03

    Compliance is not an afterthought

    FCA status, risk warnings, drawdown limits, and the inherent uncertainty of trading are stated clearly. We're not regulated advisors and we don't pretend to be.

  4. 04

    Direct contact, no layers

    You speak to the team that runs the strategies. No account managers, no sales pipeline, no scripted call. If we can't be useful on the call, we'll tell you who can.

Compliance

What we are, and what we are not.

Excelsior Consultancy provides automated trading software and consulting services. We are not authorised or regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority. We do not provide regulated investment advice. Trading carries the risk of capital loss; past performance does not guarantee future results. Any decision to engage our services should be taken with full awareness of these realities.

If you require regulated financial advice on whether algorithmic or systematic trading is suitable for you, consult an FCA-authorised financial adviser before booking.
