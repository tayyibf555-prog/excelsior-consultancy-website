02 / Account management

Cloud automation for live and prop firm accounts.

Subscription service. The strategy executes on our cloud; you keep your broker credentials. Trades mirror across the account you nominate.

How it works

One subscription connects one account. Additional accounts can be added on the same strategy with separate execution. The set-up takes place on the onboarding call so the integration is verified live before anything trades.

  • Single-account connection automated on our cloud
  • Compatible with prop-funded accounts in the $50k to $300k range
  • Same applies to live cash accounts at your existing broker
  • Clients retain credentials; we attach the strategy and trades mirror across

Supported brokers

TradeStation, Tradovate, Webull UK, Interactive Brokers, NinjaTrader. Connection happens via TraderPost. Other brokers possible on request.
